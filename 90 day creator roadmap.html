Content Formulas — Jo Viral Hote Hain
In 3 formats ko rotate karo — har din different type post karo

Type A — Shocking Comparison MOST VIRAL

Mumbai mein ₹1 Crore mein sirf 325 sq ft milta hai. Same ₹1 Crore mein Jaipur mein 2500 sq ft ka bungalow milta hai. City choose karo wisely. 🏠

→ Controversy + surprise = share hota hai. Logo ko argue karna pasand hai comments mein — algorithm ko yahi chahiye.

Type B — Hidden Fact SAVES AATE HAIN

Zomato ka pehla office ek 1BHK flat tha. Pehle employee ne 6 mahine bina salary ke kaam kiya. Aaj company 1.8 lakh crore ki hai. Shuru karna zaroori hai, perfect hona nahi. 💡

→ Saves zyada aate hain. Algorithm saves ko reach ke liye sabse zyada value deta hai — is format pe focus karo.

Type C — List / Guide Format BRAND DEALS

Property lene se pehle ye 5 cheezein check karo: 1. RERA registered hai ya nahi 2. Builder ka track record 3. Location ka future plan 4. Hidden charges list 5. Loan pre-approval Save karo — kaam aayega 📌

→ "Save karo" = saves boost. Brands is type pe sponsor karna pasand karte hain kyunki audience genuinely engaged hoti hai.

📩 Template 1 — Local Builder ko DM (5K+ followers pe)

Namaste [Builder Name] ji, Main [tumhara naam] hoon — @[page_name] Instagram page run karta hoon jo real estate facts aur property advice ke baare mein hai. Mere page par abhi [X] followers hain jinmein zyaadatar [city name] ke property buyers aur investors hain. Aapke [project name] ke baare mein ek post karna chahta hoon jisse aapke potential buyers tak reach ho sake. Ek promotional post ki fee ₹[amount] hai. Kya aap interested hain? Dhanyawad 🙏

💡 5K-10K followers pe ₹1,500-3,000 maango. 10K-25K pe ₹3,000-8,000. Local builders almost haan karte hain — unhe Instagram ki zaroorat hai aur budget hota hai.

📩 Template 2 — Tata Motors / Big Brand Email

Hello [Brand] Social Media Team, I run @vishtechcars — India's growing automotive review page with [X] followers focused on car buyers in the ₹15-35 lakh segment. Your team recently liked one of our posts, which inspired me to reach out. We create high-quality AI-assisted car reviews and have an engaged audience of genuine car buyers. We'd love to explore collaboration — test drive opportunity, sponsored review, or brand partnership. Please find our media kit attached. Looking forward to connecting! [Tumhara naam] vishtechcars.com

💡 Email better hai Instagram DM se big brands ke liye. LinkedIn pe "social media manager @tatamotors" dhundho aur direct email karo.

📩 Template 3 — Same Size Pages Collab Request

Hey [page name]! 👋 Tumhara content bahut achha hai — especially [specific post mention karo]. Main bhi similar niche mein hoon — @[page_name] — abhi [X] followers hain. Ek dusre ke posts share karein? Dono ke followers grow honge bina kisi cost ke. Interested ho toh reply karo 🤝

💡 3-5 similar size pages ko ye bhejo. 2-3 haan karenge. Free cross-promotion sabse effective early growth strategy hai — zero cost.
