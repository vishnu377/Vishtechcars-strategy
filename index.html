Ek Perfect Blog mein Kya Hona Chahiye

  • 📌
    Title (H1): Car ka naam + year + price mention + question/emotion — "Maruti WagonR 2026 — ₹5.5 Lakh mein Kya Milta Hai?"
  • 📋
    Introduction (100-150 words): Car ka short intro, buyer ke liye kyun relevant hai
  • 💰
    Price & Variants Table: Saare variants ki ex-showroom price — yeh sabse zyada search hota hai
  • ⚡
    Key Specifications: Engine, mileage, dimensions, safety rating
  • ✅
    Pros & Cons: Honest 5-7 points each — Google ko yeh original content lagta hai
  • 🆚
    Competitor Comparison: 1-2 rival cars se compare karo with internal links
  • 👥
    Buyer's Verdict: Kis type ke buyer ke liye hai — family, first car, city use, highway
  • ❓
    FAQ Section (5-7 questions): Google Featured Snippet ke liye — "WagonR mileage kitna hai?", "WagonR petrol ya CNG?"
  • 🔗
    Internal Links (2-3): Related cars ke pages pe link daalo

AdSense Approve Kaise Karo

Reject Reason: "Low Value Content" — Matlab AI Content Detect Hua

Google ka AI detector bahut smart hai. ChatGPT/Claude ka copy-paste content 90% cases mein detect hota hai. Abhi jo purane AI blogs hain unhe rewrite karna hoga.

AdSense Approval Checklist

  • 📄
    Minimum 30-40 quality pages hone chahiye — Sirf wo pages count honge jo 800+ words ke hon aur manually likhe hon
  • 📅
    Website 3+ mahine purani honi chahiye — Teri website January se hai ✅ — yeh condition poori hai
  • 📜
    Privacy Policy page — Tera hai ya nahi? Agar nahi to banao — Google required karta hai
  • 📞
    About Us + Contact page — Properly filled hone chahiye — naam, email, address
  • 🚫
    Zero AI content — Sabhi AI-generated blogs ya to delete karo ya manually rewrite karo
  • 🖼️
    Copyright free images only — Kisi bhi manufacturer ya news site se images copy mat karo — legal issue + AdSense reject
  • 📱
    Mobile friendly website — Google mobile-first index karta hai — teri site already responsive hai ✅
  • ⚡
    Page speed — WebP images use karo (tera site already kar raha hai ✅)
  • 🔗
    No broken links — Har link kaam karna chahiye — broken links = bad signal
